Biography

Kanye Omari West was born June 8, 1977. West's parents divorced when he was 3, so Kanye was forced to move to Chicago with his mother, Dr. Donda C. West. West did well in school, and excelled in the arts. During Wests elementary school years, he developed a passion for poetry, drawing and music. Most importantly however, Kanye began rapping in third grade. When Kanye was ten, he moved to Nanjing, China with his mother, due to a new teaching opportunity for his mother at Nanjing University. AFter this, Kanye returned to Chicago to study at Chicago State University, but dropped out at age 20 to pursue a career in music. West's career in music took of when he dropped his album "The College Dropout". Since the release of " The College Dropout" Kanye West has been one of the most succesful and popular rappers. His success in music led to an on going collaboration with Adidas and other major brands. West has over 160 million record sales, 22 grammies, and continues to create beautiful works of art through his music.
